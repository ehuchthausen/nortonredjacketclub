About Us

The Nor-Ton Red Jacket Club, Inc. was established in 1984. Our mission is to operate and maintain Donald F. Miller Park in order to provide low cost outdoor educational activities for area youth. The Nor-Ton Red Jacket Club, Inc. is an all-volunteer organization, committed to educating young people in health and fitness, leadership and teamwork, and an appreciation for the environment.

The Nor-Ton Red Jacket Club, Inc. has established strong affiliations with local schools, girl and boy scouts, and other youth organizations. The club offers these groups the opportunity to use the park, year round, for evening meetings, daytime programs, and weekend retreats. Recently, the Club began offering free use of the Camp to groups of children with special needs.

Uncle John's Cabin

This cabin has an open floor plan, making it roomy for large groups. It contains 30 bunks, tables, chairs, an 8 burner stove with 2 ovens, 2 refrigerators, pots, pans & cooking utensils, hot & cold running water, forced air heat and a TV & VCR/DVD player. Outside, there is a food grilling area, and a campfire area.

Thelma & Walter Balling Cabin

This cabin can be divided into three rooms, with two separate sleeping areas, making it well suited for mixed ages or genders. It contains 30 bunks, tables, chairs, a gas stove with oven, 2 refrigerators, pots, pans, & cooking utensils, hot & cold running water, forced air heat and a TV & VCR/DVD player. Outside, there is a food grilling area, and a campfire area.
